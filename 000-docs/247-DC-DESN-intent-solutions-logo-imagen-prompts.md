## Variant 1: Infrastructure Architect Badge

### Imagen 4 Prompt

```
Ultra-professional minimalist logo badge for "INTENT SOLUTIONS IO" - independent AI consulting and cloud infrastructure engineering company.

DESIGN CONCEPT:
Abstract geometric representation of multi-layer cloud infrastructure architecture. Visualize distributed systems, microservices orchestration, and data flow through intelligent agent networks.

VISUAL STRUCTURE:
- Central icon: Modular geometric composition suggesting layered infrastructure
- Three stacked or nested hexagons representing cloud layers (data, compute, orchestration)
- Subtle connection lines between layers showing data flow and agent communication
- Letter "I" or "IS" monogram integrated into architectural structure
- Clean sans-serif wordmark "INTENT SOLUTIONS IO" below or alongside icon

STYLE SPECIFICATIONS:
- Geometric precision: Sharp angles, perfect symmetry, architectural exactness
- Infrastructure abstraction: Not literal cloud icons - pure geometric representation
- Modular composition: Pieces fit together like microservices architecture
- Forward momentum: Subtle directional flow (left-to-right or bottom-to-top)
- Scalable design: Must work at 16px (favicon) and 4096px (presentation)

COLOR PALETTE:
- Primary shape: Deep charcoal #18181b (80% opacity for depth layering)
- Connection lines: Google Cloud blue #4285F4 (subtle accent, 60% opacity)
- Background: Transparent
- Typography: Charcoal #18181b (100% opacity, bold weight)

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Font style: Geometric sans-serif (Inter, Geist, or similar)
- "INTENT SOLUTIONS" in bold weight, clean tracking
- "IO" as compact technical suffix (slightly smaller, same weight)
- All caps or title case (test both for best balance)

MOOD & POSITIONING:
- Enterprise-grade technical authority
- Independent builder, not corporate consulting firm
- "Creating industries that don't exist" - category creation, not incremental
- Production-ready infrastructure, shipping to live systems
- Google Cloud ecosystem alignment (Vertex AI, BigQuery, Firebase)

REFERENCE AESTHETIC:
- Google Cloud product logos (clean, authoritative, cloud-native)
- Stripe (developer-first enterprise credibility)
- Vercel (modern platform engineering, minimalist)
- Temporal (distributed systems infrastructure, geometric precision)
- NOT generic consulting logos - this is deep infrastructure engineering

TECHNICAL CONSTRAINTS:
- Vector-ready output (clean edges, no gradients)
- Transparent background (PNG with alpha channel)
- Minimum size: 16x16px (favicon clarity required)
- Maximum scale: Infinite (SVG-convertible)
- Color modes: Works in monochrome (black on white, white on black)

OUTPUT FORMAT:
- Square composition (1:1 aspect ratio)
- 4096 x 4096 pixels minimum resolution
- Clean transparent background
- Icon + wordmark lockup (horizontal preferred)
- Badge-only variant (icon without text) for compact contexts

AVOID:
- Generic cloud icons (fluffy clouds, weather symbols)
- Overused tech symbols (gears, circuits, lightbulbs)
- Corporate consulting aesthetics (blue suits, handshakes, arrows)
- Gradient overlays, drop shadows, or glossy effects
- Literal AI imagery (brains, neural networks, robots)
```

## Brand Consistency Guidelines

### Logo Usage
- **Minimum size**: 32px height for wordmark, 16px for icon-only
- **Clear space**: Minimum padding equal to height of "I" in wordmark
- **Backgrounds**: Works on white, dark charcoal, or Google Cloud blue
- **Color modes**: Always use brand colors (no arbitrary color changes)

### Forbidden Modifications
- ❌ No stretching or distorting proportions
- ❌ No gradient overlays or effects
- ❌ No rotating or tilting the logo
- ❌ No combining with other logos or graphics
- ❌ No changing the typeface or letter spacing

### Approved Variations
- ✅ Monochrome (all black or all white)
- ✅ Horizontal or vertical lockups
- ✅ Icon-only badge for compact contexts
- ✅ Light mode and dark mode variants
